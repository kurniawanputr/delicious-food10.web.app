The ingredients needed to make BreadPeanutButterCookies:
  1. Prepare 1 cup peanut butter
  2. Get 2/3 cup powder sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup oil
  4. Take 6 pieces breads
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp cco chips
Steps to make BreadPeanutButterCookies:
  1. Cut the side of the bread&griend it to makes breadcrumbs.
  2. Take a bowl add bread breadcrumbs,peanut butter,oil,sugar,&mix it nicely.Makes a dough.
  3. Preheat the microwave on 180 degree for 5 mints.
  4. Shape the cookies from dough &put choco chips on top.Than bake it 3mints on preheated microwave.
  5. Bread cookies is ready.
