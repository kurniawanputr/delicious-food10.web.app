The ingredients needed to make Bacon ham roll:
  1. Prepare 2 lb ground beef
  2. Prepare 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 1 cup breadcrumbs
  4. Take to taste Salt
  5. Take to taste Pepper
  6. Make ready 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  7. Take 4 slices ham
  8. Make ready 8 slices cheddar cheese
  9. Get 2 cups spinach
  10. Prepare 10 slices bacon

Steps to make Bacon ham roll:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350¬∞F (180¬∞C)
  2. In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, eggs, breadcrumbs, salt, pepper and garlic powder. Mix until wel combined.
  3. Put the mixture in a medium baking tray lined with aluminum foil and distribute the meat mixture evenly.
  4. Add the ham on the meat mixture. Put the cheese on the ham. Put the spinach on the cheese.
  5. Fearfully lift the meat and roll the meat up.
  6. Place straps of bacon on an other baking tray lined with aluminum foil.
  7. Place the meat roll on the bacon and roll the bacon an aluminum foil around the meat roll.
  8. Bake the aluminum wrapped roll for 25 minutes.
  9. Increase the temperature to 500¬∞F (260¬∞C). Remove the foil and bake for 25 more minutes.
