The ingredients needed to make Lamb biryani:
  1. Prepare 1 tablespoon chilli powder
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon onion powder
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon cardamom pods
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon cloves
  6. Take 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  8. Prepare 3 bay leaves
  9. Take 1 teaspoon garam masala
  10. Get 2 tablespoons garlic Chilli sauce
  11. Prepare 1 teaspoon turmeric
  12. Prepare 3 cinnamon sticks
  13. Make ready 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  14. Get 1/2 teaspoon saffron
  15. Get 4 tablespoons yogurt
  16. Prepare 1/4 cup oil
  17. Get cube Seasoning
  18. Take Handful chop coriander and mint
  19. Prepare Salt
  20. Take Lamb
  21. Get 3 cups Basmati rice
  22. Make ready 1 cup deep fried onions
  23. Take 1/2 cup chopped onions
Steps to make Lamb biryani:
  1. Start by washing and soaking your basmati rice in a cold water for 20 minutes
  2. Wash and cut your meat and set aside. Then add all the spices and seasoning and 1/3 cup of the fried onion and chopped coriander and mint leaves then the yogurt and mix. Allow it to marinate for at least two hours then cook till very soft
  3. Now cook the rice with the bag leaves, another tablespoon of chili sauce five cardamom, cinnamon and cloves and cumin seeds cook the rice till almost done
  4. Drain the rice and set aside
  5. In another pot and oil and half of the lamb masala then half of the rice
  6. Add the fried onions and the remaining coriander and mint leaves followed by the remaining rice and sauce cover and allow it to cook don-not turn
  7. Once the rice is fully done take it off the heat and then slightly
  8. Serve with fresh coriander leaves and fried onions
