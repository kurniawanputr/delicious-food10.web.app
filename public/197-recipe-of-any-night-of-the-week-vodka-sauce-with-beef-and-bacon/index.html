The ingredients needed to make Vodka sauce with beef and bacon:
  1. Prepare 4 strips bacon
  2. Get 1 1/2 lb ground beef
  3. Take 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  4. Make ready 15 oz can fired roasted tomatoes
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup vodka
  6. Get 3/4 cup heavy cream
  7. Take 1 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1 tsp pepper
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
  10. Make ready 1 large onion chopped
  11. Make ready 2 cloves garlic chopped
  12. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  13. Get 5 cups cooked pasta

Steps to make Vodka sauce with beef and bacon:
  1. Fry the bacon. Remove and set aside to cool. And crumble
  2. Brown the ground beef in the bacon grease.
  3. While the beef is cooking, chop the onion and garlic
  4. Drain most of the grease. But leave just enough to cook the onion and garlic until tender.
  5. Add the cans of tomatoes, vodka, salt, pepper, red pepper, bay leaf, and crumbled bacon
  6. Simmer for 20 min
  7. Add the heavy cream
  8. Simmer for 10 min
  9. Remove bay leaf
  10. Add to cooked pasta & enjoy!
