The ingredients needed to make Lamb Chops:
  1. Take 1-1/2 pound lamb chops (6)
  2. Make ready 1/2 stick butter
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  5. Take 1 tablespoon rosemary infused oil see recipe
  6. Get 1/2 cup chopped olives mixed
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup Cabernet Sauvignon merlot
Instructions to make Lamb Chops:
  1. Add oil to a hot pan, and sear on each side of chops 3 minutes
  2. Add butter, olives, and season the chops.
  3. Add merlot. Let simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Let rest 10 minutes covered.
  5. Serve I hope you enjoy!
