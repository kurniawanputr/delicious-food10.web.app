The ingredients needed to make Egg Twist Bread #BreadMaking101 #HomeBreadMakingGang😇😊:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  2. Take 1 cup warm milk
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. Get 1 1/2 tablespoon yeast
  5. Prepare 3 large eggs (2 to mix dough and 1 for egg wash.)
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup butter (room temperature)
  7. Take 3 cup flour (plus 1 extra for kneading and for dusting on pan)
Steps to make Egg Twist Bread #BreadMaking101 #HomeBreadMakingGang😇😊:
  1. Warm milk, pour in bowl,add in the salt, sugar then stir the mixture, add in the yeast, allow the yeast to prove for 5-7 mins.
  2. Add the melted butter, add eggs, stir well until it is all blended together.
  3. Gradually add in the flour,1 cup at a time. Next knead the dough with your hands until air is well incorporated in. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and continue to knead for 7 mins until smooth and elastic. Do not over add flour, so the bread won't be dry.
  4. Place the dough in a grease bowl and cover it. Allow the dough to rest and double in size for 60 mins. After 1 hr place the dough on a floured surface, knead and cut into three balls and roll them out.
  5. Rolled each piece out to make braided rolls. Next braid them together. This dough will give you two set of braided dough..
  6. Carefully place the dough on a greased baking sheet and next plavdvtgd second braided roll on top the first…. brush the top with egg wash…cover again and allow to rise for another 30mins.
  7. After 30 mins bake bread for 25mins or till golden brown.. once bread is ready set on a baking rack to cool, slice and enjoy
