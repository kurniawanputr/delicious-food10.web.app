The ingredients needed to make Cheesy malunggay bread:
  1. Make ready 2 cups bread flour, 1 cup sugar
  2. Take 150 ml water or milk
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Get 1/2 cup oil or butter
  5. Take 1 and half tsp yeast and bread improver
  6. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Get 1 cup malunggay leaves
  8. Get Toppings
  9. Prepare 1 cup grated cheese
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup milk powder
  11. Get 1/2 cup brown sugar
Instructions to make Cheesy malunggay bread:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients (flour,yeast,sugar, baking powder)
  2. Let the yeast be activated in walter or milk for 10mins
  3. Add egg, oil/melted butter and activated yeast to dry ingredients
  4. Combine to form a dough, mix with malunggay leaves
  5. Dust the working surface with flour and knead your dough for 10 minutes, after that put it on a dry container with a cover until proofed (doubled in size).
  6. When it is proofed knead again for 5minutes then let it rest again for 15 minutes
  7. Now it's time to prepare the toppings.
  8. Heat the powdered milk and sugar on a sauce pan with 15ml water until the sugar melts, then let it cool.
  9. Go back to your dough, knead again for 5minutes. Cut it in even pieces, then shape it into round buns. Place them on your greased baking tray.

  11. Pour over the topping sauce on your ready to bake dough, spread it evenly and lastly sprinkle the grated cheese on top. Bake it for 20 minutes at 300Â°F.
