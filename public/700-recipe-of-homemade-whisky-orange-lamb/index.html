The ingredients needed to make Whisky Orange Lamb:
  1. Get 250 gm lamb
  2. Get 1 teaspoon Greek yoghurt
  3. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
  4. Get Half small Spanish onion
  5. Make ready 1 piece ginger
  6. Take leaves Some dill
  7. Make ready Italian herbs
  8. Prepare Salt
  9. Prepare Black pepper
  10. Take Orange peel
  11. Take Strawberries
  12. Make ready Fried onions
  13. Take 15 ml Jack Daniel's Whiskey
  14. Prepare Greens (broccoli, beans, carrot)
  15. Take Olive oil
Instructions to make Whisky Orange Lamb:
  1. Marinate the lamb with some olive oil, dill, salt,pepper, Greek yoghurt and thinly cut orange peel. Use some cinnamon and chilli as per taste. Let it rest for 15 mins
  2. Place a pan on stove and let it heat
  3. Put some olive oil and let it sit for a 10-15 seconds
  4. Place the marinated lamb on pan
  5. Place the onion, smashed garlic clove and sliced ginger on pan
  6. Sprinkle 15 ml Jack Daniel's Whiskey (or any Irish whiskey of choice)
  7. Let one side cook until golden brown
  8. Turn the lamb and cook until golden brown
  9. When cooked, place on a plate
  10. Sprinkle fried onions around the lamb on plate
  11. Skewer fresh strawberry on the chopstick for garnish
  12. Sprinkle some Italian herbs for garnish
  13. Stir-fry greens and serve with the lamb
