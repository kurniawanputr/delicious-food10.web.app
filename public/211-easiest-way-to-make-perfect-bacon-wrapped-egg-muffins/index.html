The ingredients needed to make Bacon Wrapped Egg Muffins:
  1. Prepare eggs
  2. Take Bacon (strips)
  3. Get salt (ground)
  4. Make ready salt
  5. Take cheese
  6. Get cupcake tray
  7. Take bread
  8. Make ready baking spray
Steps to make Bacon Wrapped Egg Muffins:
  1. Spray inside of cupcake tray
  2. Cut bread into small circles and place in bottom of tray
  3. Place bacon on insides of tray
  4. Crack the egg in the center of cupcake tray in between bacon
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top
  6. Sprinle seasoning (salt and pepper)
  7. You can add any other optional toppings
  8. Bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes
  9. You can crack an egg in a bowl and pix it then put it in if you want it to bake like scrambled eggs
