The ingredients needed to make Smoked bacon wrapped green beans:
  1. Get 1.5 pounds green beans
  2. Prepare 4 strips bacon
  3. Prepare 3 tablespoons butter
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup brown sugar
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon cracked pepper
  7. Get Garlic

Steps to make Smoked bacon wrapped green beans:
  1. Bring large pot of water to boil. Place green beans in pot and boil for 8 minutes
  2. In large bowl combine butter, brown sugar, garlic, green beans, and soy sauce and mix
  3. Wrap 8 to 10 green beans tightly with bacon. Sprinkle each bundle with pepper
  4. Heat grill to 400. Grill bundles for 10 minutes or until bacon is crispy
