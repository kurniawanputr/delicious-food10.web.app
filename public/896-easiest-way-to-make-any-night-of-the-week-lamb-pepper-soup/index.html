The ingredients needed to make Lamb pepper soup:
  1. Take 1 kg lamb meat
  2. Prepare 4 potatoes
  3. Take 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  4. Get leaf Bay
  5. Make ready stick Cinnamon
  6. Take Seasoning
  7. Make ready Garlic powder
  8. Get Ginger powder
  9. Make ready Curry
  10. Get leaves Mint
  11. Take 2 cooking spoon oil
  12. Take Onion and bonnet paste
Steps to make Lamb pepper soup:
  1. Wash the meat.. add the spices and seasonings, slice onion. Put on heat and cook for 40mins
  2. Add the pastes, the bay leaf, the mint, cinnamon, oil and potatoes, if theres need to add seasoning you add.
  3. Cover for 2 minutes and then remove the cinnamon stick, cover and cook for 15 mins.
  4. Done
