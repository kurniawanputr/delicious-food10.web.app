The ingredients needed to make BBQ Roast leg of lamb:
  1. Get Leg of Lamb
  2. Get 4 Cloves garlic minced
  3. Get 1 Table spoon fresh Rosemary
  4. Take 1 Table spoon fresh Thyme
  5. Take 1 Table spoon Dijon mustard
  6. Get 1 Table spoon Olive oil
  7. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
Instructions to make BBQ Roast leg of lamb:
  1. Pre heat the BBQ/ Oven for indirect cooking at 170c.
  2. Score the top of the meat with a sharp knife and push garlic Cloves into the meat then pat dry with kitchen roll.
  3. In a small bowl combine all the ingredients and mix together.
  4. Place the lamb in the roasting tin and smother with the ingredients making sure to thoroughly cover the meat.
  5. Place in the BBQ and roast it untill you reach an internal temprature of 60-66c for medium. Around 1 hour and 30 minutes at a temprature of 170c. I add a bit of smoking wood at this point to add extra flavour.
