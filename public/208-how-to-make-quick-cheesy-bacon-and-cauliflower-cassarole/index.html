The ingredients needed to make Cheesy,bacon and cauliflower cassarole:
  1. Make ready 1 large cauliflower
  2. Make ready 200 g cubbed smoked bacon
  3. Get 1 and 1/2 cup grated cheese - cheddar or similar
  4. Get 150 g cream cheese
  5. Make ready 200 ml heavy cream
  6. Get 1/4 cup spring onion
  7. Take Parsley - optional
  8. Get to taste Salt n pepper

You'll love making this recipe this fall! You definitely want to cook the cauliflower a little first so that it doesn't take forever in the oven. Steps to make Cheesy,bacon and cauliflower cassarole:
  1. First chop and steam the cauliflower,also in a pan fry the bacon set aside for later.
  2. Pre-heat oven 210' into a mixing bowl put in the cream cheese,heavy cream,spring onion,most of the grated cheese (save a handful for later),bacon,cauliflower from before salt n pepper to taste combine together well.
  3. Place mixture into an oven proof dish,sprinkle remaining grated cheese and parsley (if using) cook for 20 mins. (Should have slight golden colour on top.)
