The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Brionche Bread:
  1. Take 530 g all purpose flour
  2. Take 10 g instant yeast
  3. Get 175 ml warm water
  4. Make ready 3/4 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 100 g sugar
  6. Get 3 large egg
  7. Prepare 150 g butter
  8. Take Chocolate spread
  9. Take 180 g dark chocolate
  10. Take 120 g butter
  11. Take 80 g powdered sugar
  12. Make ready 42 g cocoa powder
  13. Prepare Small pinch of salt
Instructions to make Chocolate Brionche Bread:
  1. Mix all the ingredienta in a bowl and stir slowly until combine in a mixer. Then increase the speed for 8 min.
  2. Cover the dough and let it rest in a fridge for at least 8 hours or overnight.
  3. Melt the butter and dark chocolate. Pour powdered sugar and cocoa powder and mix them well until no lump.
  4. Divide the dough into 4. For each dough, roll it until it spread evenly. Then spread the chocolate mixture equally.
  5. Fold the dough 2 times and cut it into 8. Braided the dough nicely. Let it rise for another 1 hour or double it size.
  6. Bake the bread for 20 min at 180C. Enjoy!
