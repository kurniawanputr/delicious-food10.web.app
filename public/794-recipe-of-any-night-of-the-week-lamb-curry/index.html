The ingredients needed to make Lamb Curry:
  1. Get 1 Kg Lean Lamb Shoulder Diced (I deboned a shoulder joint myself as its much cheaper, but all supermarkets sell diced lamb)
  2. Take Juice of 1 lemon
  3. Make ready 1 large heaped tbsp. of garam masala
  4. Take 5 cloves or garlic roughly chopped
  5. Take 1 large knob of ginger grated
  6. Get 2 tbsp. vegetable oil or fry light
  7. Make ready 1 tin tomatoes
  8. Take 1 large onion sliced
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp. cumin
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp. ground corriander
  11. Get 1 tbsp. turmeric
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp. smoked hot paprika
  13. Take 1 whole chilli sliced in half (up to you which variety for your desired heat)
  14. Take 1 cup water to add to make up the desired consistency sauce
  15. Get 2-3 tbsp. low fat yogurt (I used Skyr)
  16. Make ready Salt and Pepper a good pinch
  17. Make ready 2 tbsp. fresh roughly chopped corriander
Steps to make Lamb Curry:
  1. First of all put the diced lamb in a large bowl, pour over the lemon juice and mix in the salt, pepper and garam masala. Leave to marinate for as long as possible preferably over night (although I only had an hour and it was still delicious).
  2. Whilst the lamb pieces are marinating prepare the spices and vegetables. Once ready, get a large frying pan and heat on a medium to high heat and add the spices to heat through, this only takes a minute or two.
