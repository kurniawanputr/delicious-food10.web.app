The ingredients needed to make Summer Fresh Corn Chowder (with Bacon):
  1. Get 1/2 lb bacon, diced
  2. Make ready 1 medium onion, diced small
  3. Get 2 celery, diced small
  4. Get 1 large carrot, diced small
  5. Get 6 ears fresh corn
  6. Prepare 3 cups reserved "corn water" -stock
  7. Make ready 2 cloves garlic, minced
  8. Prepare 1.5 cups light cream (or half and half works)
  9. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  10. Make ready 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  11. Take 1 tbs corn starch made into slurry with about 1/3 cup water
Steps to make Summer Fresh Corn Chowder (with Bacon):
  1. In a pot, boil ears of corn in water. Once cooked, reserve 3 cups of the water and cut the corn off the cobs and seg aside.
  2. In the same pot(now that you've dumped everything out) crisp your bacon and set aside to drain.
  3. In the SAME pot (1 pot wonder, here) saute your onion, garlic, potatoes, celery, carrots until the onions are translucent and veggies are glistening.
  4. Add your thyme and salt and pepper to taste and now stir in your corn.
  5. Pour in your reserved corn water. Bring everything to a boil, then reduce heat to medium/low.
  6. Once potatoes are cooked through and veggies are soft, SLOWLY stir in your cream. Then stir in your cornstarch slurry.
  7. Let everything come to a boil again, then reduce to low for 20 min
