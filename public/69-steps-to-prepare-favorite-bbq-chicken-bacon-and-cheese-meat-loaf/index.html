The ingredients needed to make Bbq chicken bacon and cheese meat loaf:
  1. Prepare 3 lb ground chicken
  2. Prepare 5 oz pack of bacon bits
  3. Take 3 eggs
  4. Take 2 (6 oz) package garlic buttered Ritz crackers, crushed
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoons rotisserie chicken seasoning from McCormick
  6. Make ready 1 cup sauteed onion and celery
  7. Prepare 8 oz Cubed cheddar cheese
  8. Make ready Open pit thick and tangy bbq sauce

Steps to make Bbq chicken bacon and cheese meat loaf:
  1. In a large bowl mix your ground chicken, crushed crackers, egg, seasoning, bacon bits, and sauted onions and celery. Take the cubed cheddar cheese and mix one by one in the mixture. Try and not break the cheese.
  2. Pour the mixture in a 13×9 pan and form into a loaf. Bake at temperature 375 for an hr. Durning that hour the last 15 to 20 minutes left base your loaf with the bbq sauce. You want to base it liberally. Base with the bbq all over, even the sides of the loaf.
