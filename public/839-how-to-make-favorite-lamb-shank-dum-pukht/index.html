The ingredients needed to make Lamb shank dum pukht:
  1. Take 2 lamb shank
  2. Make ready Half kg mutton
  3. Make ready 3 tomatoes
  4. Get 3 potatoes
  5. Get 3 onions
  6. Get 4-5 green chilli
  7. Take Salt to taste
  8. Get 1 tablespoon Cooking oil
  9. Take Ata patila cover karni ke liye
Steps to make Lamb shank dum pukht:
  1. Aik patile me oil lamb shank mutton dal ke oper potatoes tomatoes onions green chilli Ar salt dar ly ab patile Au aty se ache tarha cover karly
  2. After 4 hrs patile ko open kar ke 5 mint ke liye tez anj py raky ta ke is ke pani Khosh hojahe
  3. The simple and tests lamb shank dum Bukhara is ready
