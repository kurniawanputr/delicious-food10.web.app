The ingredients needed to make Napa Cabbage & Bok Choy with Bacon:
  1. Get 1/2 head Napa cabbage
  2. Get 1 small bok choy
  3. Make ready 4 slices thick cut bacon
  4. Get 1 tbs soy sauce
  5. Prepare 1 tbs hoisin sauce
  6. Get 2 tsp honey
  7. Get 1 tsp ginger paste (or finely minced fresh ginger)
  8. Get 1 tsp sesame oil
  9. Make ready 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes
Instructions to make Napa Cabbage & Bok Choy with Bacon:
  1. In a small bowl wisk together soy sauce, hoisin sauce, honey, ginger, sesame oil, and pepper flakes. Set aside.
  2. Cut bacon to 1/4"strips. Cut green portions of cabbage and bok choy from the white portions. Slice whites into 1/4" pieces, greens into 1/2" pieces. Set green pieces aside for later.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook bacon until crisp and fat has rendered. Remove bacon to a paper towel lined plate leaving fat behind in skillet.
  4. Add whites of cabbage and bok choy to skillet. Cook until just tender, stirring frequently. About 5-7 minutes.
  5. Return bacon to skillet. Add greens and sauce. Stir to combine. Cook just until greens wilt and sauce heats through. About 2 minutes.
  6. Serve as-is for a delicious side or over rice as a main dish. Enjoy!
