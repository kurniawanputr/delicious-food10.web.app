The ingredients needed to make Bread rolls:
  1. Make ready Slice bread
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 3 tablespoon powder milk
  4. Take Sugar
  5. Take Strawberry jam
Instructions to make Bread rolls:
  1. Cut the edges of the bread
  2. Use rolling pin to flat the bread
  3. Spread the jam on the bread
  4. Roll the bread and slead the end with water
  5. Mix your milk eggs and sugar with water
  6. Coat your bread inside the mixure
  7. Spray your pan with butter and arrange your bread roll cook both sides in a low heat
