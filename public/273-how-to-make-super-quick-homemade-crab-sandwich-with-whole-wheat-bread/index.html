The ingredients needed to make Crab sandwich with whole wheat bread:
  1. Prepare 6 crab sticks
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  3. Get 1 1/2 tablespoon ketchup
  4. Get 1/2 tablespoon lime
  5. Get Salt and pepper
  6. Take Lettuce
  7. Make ready 3 medium tomatoes
  8. Make ready 1/4 of an onion
  9. Take 1 avocado
  10. Prepare Balsamic vinegar
