The ingredients needed to make Bacon Pepperoni Pasta Bake:
  1. Take 1 box elbow noodle pasta
  2. Take 1 jar your favorite pizza sauce
  3. Take slices Pepperoni
  4. Get 1 lb cooked crumbled bacon
  5. Prepare Shredded mozzarella cheese
  6. Make ready Additional pizza toppings of your choice
Steps to make Bacon Pepperoni Pasta Bake:
  1. Cook bacon, place on paper towel and set aside. Boil pan of water, add teaspoon of salt to water. Add pasta and boil until noodles are tender. Drain pasta.
  2. Put pasta back into pan and add your pizza sauce. Crumble bacon.
  3. Place half of the mixture into a casserole dish. Top it with half of the crumbled bacon and pepperoni slices, top that layer with cheese. Repeat layering noodles, bacon, pepperoni and cheese again.
