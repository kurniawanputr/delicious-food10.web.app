The ingredients needed to make Chicken Bacon Sundried Tomato Pasta:
  1. Take 1 (16 oz) bag penne pasta
  2. Get 1/2 package bacon
  3. Take 1 (8 oz) jar sun dried tomatoes in oil
  4. Make ready 1 (8 oz) bag shredded white cheddar cheese
  5. Prepare Garlic powder for seasoning
  6. Get 2 chicken breasts or 4 chicken cutlets
  7. Take 2 tbs butter
  8. Get 2 green onions/scallions
Steps to make Chicken Bacon Sundried Tomato Pasta:
  1. Cook chicken (or you can use pre-cooked). I usually boil water with a little bit of chicken broth & garlic & herb seasoning. When water is boiled, add chicken & cook 20 minutes. Start cooking bacon over medium heat. Boil water for pasta & cook 1 minute less than package says. Once chicken is cooked, remove & cube.
  2. Once bacon is almost done, add chicken l, Sundried tomatoes, butter & green onion, sprinkle garlic powder. Saute until chicken is warm & bacon is crisp. optional: add 1tsp minced garlic & chop bacon
  3. Add cooked pasta to pan, then bag of cheese. Mix around. If desired add more cheese or alfredo sauce. Enjoy.
