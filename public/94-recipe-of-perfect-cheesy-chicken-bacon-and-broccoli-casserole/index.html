The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Chicken, Bacon and Broccoli Casserole:
  1. Get 6-8 chicken thighs
  2. Get 3 cups brown minute rice
  3. Make ready 2 cups crumbled bacon bits
  4. Make ready 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  5. Take 1/2 cup milk
  6. Prepare 1 package frozen broccoli flourets
  7. Make ready 1/2 tablespoon Garlic Powder
  8. Get to taste Kosher salt
  9. Prepare to taste Ground black pepper
  10. Get 1 can cream of mushroom condensed soup
  11. Get 1 can cream of chicken condensed soup
  12. Prepare 1/2 package dry ranch mix

Steps to make Cheesy Chicken, Bacon and Broccoli Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Spray a 9"x13" caserole dish with cooking spray
  3. Lay your chicken thighs down in it and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. In a large bowl mix together the soups, milk, dry ranch, garlic, salt, pepper. Then mix in the rice. Then mix in the bacon bits and shredded cheese. Then the brocolli.
  5. Dump the mix over the chicken thighs
  6. Cook uncovered for approximately 50 minutes or until chicken has reached proper internal temperature and rice is cooked through.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
