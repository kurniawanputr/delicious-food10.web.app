The ingredients needed to make Holiday French Rack of Lamb with a Pistachio Crust ğŸ‘:
  1. Take 2 racks French Lamb
  2. Take Sprinkle of Herbs De Province
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup crushed pistachio nuts
  4. Prepare 8 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Get 1 teaspoon seasoned bread crumbs
  6. Prepare Sprinkle Black Pepper
  7. Prepare Sprinkle Salt
  8. Get Olive oil
Steps to make Holiday French Rack of Lamb with a Pistachio Crust ğŸ‘:
  1. Rinse lamb in cold water then season both sides of rack of French lamb with Herbs De Province, salt, garlic and black pepper…..
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet and brown lamb in oil until lightly browned, then place lamb into a baking pan……
  3. In a bowl stir together: Crushed Pistachios, bread crumbs, 1 teaspoon olive oil……
  4. Next pat pistachio mixture onto French Lamb, just the top of your lamb…….
  5. Bake lamb in a preheated 400 degree oven for 20-25 minutes, lamb will form a beautiful crust over the top and the center will be pink……
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes, serve and enjoy ğŸ˜‰!! PERFECTO !! ğŸ˜‹
