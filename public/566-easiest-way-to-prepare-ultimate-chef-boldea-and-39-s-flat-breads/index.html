The ingredients needed to make Chef Boldea's Flat Breads:
  1. Prepare Flatbreads
  2. Get 1.5 cup flour
  3. Make ready 2 tspn baking powder
  4. Get to taste Salt
  5. Get 1 cup yogurt
  6. Get The Toppings
  7. Prepare Frank's Red Hot/Any red sauce
  8. Prepare 1 tbpsn olive oil
  9. Prepare 1 cup shredded/torn lean meat
  10. Make ready 1 cup ricotta cheese
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup part skim mozzarella cheeee
  12. Prepare 1 handful leaf veggies (optional)
  13. Prepare Mushrooms (optional)
  14. Get Onions (optional)
  15. Make ready Olives (optional)
Steps to make Chef Boldea's Flat Breads:
  1. Assemble dough ingredients and mix until a cohesive, smooth dough forms
  2. Cut into equal sections and roll out to desired width and thickness
  3. Fry until browned on both sides
  4. Preheat oven to 365°F
  5. Cook and shred meat, set to the side
  6. Put a thin layer of sauce across each flat bread and add olive oil on top
  7. Top with the desired toppings as listed in the ingredients, and add shredded meat as well
  8. Put the flat breads in the oven until the cheese melts and there is a slight browning on the surface
  9. Serve and enjoy!
