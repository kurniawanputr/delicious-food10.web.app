The ingredients needed to make Fruit cocktail:
  1. Prepare 6-8 tbsp Custard
  2. Get Milk 1 litter
  3. Take Cocktail tin 1
  4. Get 1 pack Cream
  5. Take Comel 4 tbp
  6. Get 1/4 cup Sugar
  7. Get 4 tbsp Dry fruits crushed
Instructions to make Fruit cocktail:
  1. In 1/3 cup moderate water add custard powder n mix well keep aside.
  2. Now Boil milk and add custard then stir well.
  3. Add sugar, when sugar dissolves turn off heat.
  4. Take out custard in a mixing bowl and place it into the freezer till it's cold.
  5. After that add cocktail, dry fruits, cream and comel and mix well..
